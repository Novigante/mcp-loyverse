# Sample Prompts for mcp-loyverse

These are example prompts you can use with an AI assistant connected to the mcp-loyverse MCP server.

## Sales & Analytics

- "What were today's total sales?"
- "Give me a sales summary for this week"
- "What was the average ticket amount this month?"
- "How much revenue did we make yesterday?"

## Top Selling Items

- "Show me the top 5 best-selling items this week"
- "What are the top 10 items by sales amount this month?"
- "Which items sold the most yesterday?"

## Employee Performance

- "Which employee had the highest sales this month?"
- "Show me the top 3 employees by receipt count this week"
- "Who sold the most today?"

## Receipts

- "List all receipts from yesterday"
- "How many receipts were processed today?"
- "Get the details of receipt number R-1234"
- "Show me receipts from store X for this week"

## Catalog & Resources

- "List all items in the catalog"
- "Get details for item ID abc-123"
- "Show me all employees"
- "List all stores"
- "What currency does the merchant use?"

## Customers

- "List all customers"
- "Find the customer with email john@example.com"
- "Get details for customer ID xyz-456"
